Privacy Policy for Onion Skin Draw

Last updated: 10/25/2024

We value your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information about you when you use our apps.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information directly. However, third-party services integrated into our app may collect and use the following data:

IP Address: To track your general location and for delivering ads relevant to your region.
Device ID: A unique identifier for your device may be used to serve targeted advertising.
Diagnostic Data: Information related to app performance, including crash data, may be collected to improve the app.
Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app, including actions you take within the app.

Third-Party Ad Networks

We use third-party advertising networks that may track your activity for advertising purposes. These networks may collect information such as your IP address or device ID to deliver personalized ads that match your interests. These third-party services may also collect diagnostic data, performance data, or crash data to ensure the reliability of their services.

How Your Information is Used

The data collected by third-party ad networks is primarily used for:

Serving personalized ads.
Tracking ad performance.
Improving app functionality and diagnosing technical issues.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy here.
